Introduction

Jegcy Systems Limited is a premier provider of comprehensive fire safety and low-voltage electronic security solutions. The company is dedicated to ensuring safety, security, and seamless communication through the following services:

  1. Fire Extinguishing and Detection Systems: Supply and installation of advanced fire suppression and detection systems designed to protect lives and property by ensuring timely fire detection and suppression.

  2. Intruder Detection Systems: Provision and deployment of state-of-the-art systems to safeguard properties by detecting unauthorized access and deterring potential intrusions.

  3. Public Address Systems: Installation and supply of robust public address systems for effective communication in various environments, ensuring clarity and reliability.

  4. Access Control Systems: Implementation of cutting-edge access control solutions that regulate and monitor entry to secured areas, enhancing safety and operational efficiency.

Jegcy Systems Limited is committed to delivering tailored solutions that meet client-specific needs, fostering a secure and efficient environment for businesses and individuals across Africa.

Fire Detection and Extinguishing Systems

Fires in buildings result in significant loss of life and property each year, yet many incidents can be prevented with proper fire protection measures. When fires occur, their impact can be minimized through effective detection, control systems, and well-implemented procedures.

At Jegcy Systems Limited, we specialize in providing tailored fire detection and extinguishing solutions. Backed by extensive research and innovation, our systems include advanced detection technology, integrated control platforms, and automated extinguishing solutions. Designed for industrial, commercial, and residential applications, these systems ensure rapid detection and response, reducing risks and protecting lives and assets with reliability and efficiency.

  • Conventional and Addressable Fire Alarm Sytems
  • Fire Sprinkler Systems / Hydrant lines
  • Flame and Gas Systems
  • Automated or fixed Extinguishing System (with extinguishing agents such as FM200, CO2, DCP, etc)

Intruder Detection Systems

As security requirements for facilities such as corporations, campuses, stadiums, airports, and hospitals continue to grow, controlling access has become a cornerstone of effective security strategies. Jegcy Systems Limited offers advanced Intruder Detection Systems designed to enhance safety by combining perimeter protection with modern access control solutions, addressing the rising need for proactive measures to protect lives and property.

Our systems feature a central control panel, which serves as the operational hub, supported by remote keypads for programming and management. Key components include motion detectors, panic alarm buttons, and sounders to deliver immediate alerts during an intrusion. A key advantage of our solution is its integration capability with alarm messaging systems like Commtech Messenger, enabling silent notifications to security personnel via GSM or paper-based messaging. This ensures discreet, efficient response without alerting intruders, making our systems a reliable choice for diverse security needs.

  • Motion Sensors
  • Surveillance Cameras e.g. CCTVs
