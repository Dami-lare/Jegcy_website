ABOUT US


Our Story

Jegcy Systems Limited is an indigenous company in Nigeria.

Established in 2008, Jegcy Systems Limited has been providing engineering, construction, inspections, QAQC, manpower supply, training and procurement services to both the upstream and downstream operations in engineering and technical services, materials management, and equipment supplies, industrial and logistic support. In execution of this project, it is the policy of our company that every reasonable effort shall be made to ensure that all employees have a safe and healthy place in which to work with adequate welfare facilities and that the surrounding environment shall not be adversely affected by work activities. The prevention of all accidents particularly those involving personal injury or damage to property is recognized as being essential to efficient operation.

In order to ensure safety at all levels of performance, we adopt the following strategies

  • Identification of causes of accident
  • Control of causes of accident
  • Minimization of accident
While the overall policy responsibility for safety rests on the management, all individuals at every level will have responsibility for carrying out that policy. Jegcy Systems Limited retains the services of good hospitals and clinics to cater for the employees and also ensure that adequate medical services are provided to cover all our operations. The company is committed to ensuring a clean and healthy work environment. We also take every necessary step towards minimization of work hazards such as noise, dust and other pollution agents released during our jobs.
