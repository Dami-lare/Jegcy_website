About Us

Fire Safety and Security Systems

Jegcy Systems Limited is a distinguished provider of fire safety solutions and electronic security systems, with a strong presence in Lagos and Abuja. Our company offers a comprehensive suite of services, encompassing design, engineering, project management, installation, and ongoing support. We cater to diverse sectors, offering expertise in engineering, technical services, equipment supply, and Procurement. We are committed to delivering exceptional value through our integrated approach, ensuring that our solutions meet the highest standards of quality and safety across all projects. Our strategic locations and industry expertise allow us to effectively serve both upstream and downstream operations, reinforcing our reputation as a trusted partner in fire safety and security systems.
